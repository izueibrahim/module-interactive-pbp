Holiday Destinations

This project involved forty Form 4 pupils. Choosing the best destination for a holiday is a common issue faced by many. Some families may hold discussions and make decisions together for their holiday destinations. Hence this project could develop pupilsâ€™ skills in gathering information and choosing the best details and most reliable information to justify their arguments. The project was carried out for three weeks.
